Catch Our Attention with a Headline

And add some short but punchy supporting copy!

Above the fold, you should put the stuff that'll make your reader want to scroll down and read your feature list.

Testimonials and the benefits of your product are perfect here (just remember, the benefits, not a dry feature list).

That video up there could also have been the signup form or another more tangible call to action.

Only include relevant links

You don't want your readers clicking away and getting distracted so don't link too much to other parts of your website. We're trying to build as straight a road as possible between googling for your product and clicking a 'buy' button.

But it would help to give us reasons to trust you, so if you've got security certificates, or if you're certified Fair Trade? Brag about that here and show us those well-known logos, baby!
